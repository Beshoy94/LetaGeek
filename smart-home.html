Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers to common smart home installation questions.

What smart home devices do you install?

We install a wide range of smart home devices including Ring Video Doorbells, Nest Thermostats, Arlo Security Cameras, smart locks, and smart lighting systems.

Do you provide the smart home devices or do I need to buy them?

You can purchase your own devices, or we can source them for you. We recommend buying from Amazon or directly from the manufacturer.

How long does a smart home installation take?

Most single-device installations (like a doorbell or thermostat) take about 30-60 minutes. Larger setups with multiple cameras may take 2-3 hours.
