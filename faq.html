Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the Inland Empire including Rancho Cucamonga, Ontario, Fontana, Upland, Claremont, Chino, Chino Hills, Corona, Riverside, and surrounding cities.

How do I get a quote?

You can get an instant quote using our online calculator, fill out the contact form on our website, or call/text us at (909) 257-7499.

Do I need to provide the TV bracket or smart home devices?

You can purchase your own equipment, or we can recommend and source quality products for you. We work with all major brands.

How long does a typical TV mounting take?

Most standard TV mounts take 1-2 hours. Jobs with in-wall cord concealment or fireplace mounting may take longer depending on the wall type.

Can you mount a TV on brick or stone?

Yes, we have the specialized tools and experience to mount TVs on brick, stone, concrete, and other masonry surfaces.

What smart home devices do you install?

We install Ring Video Doorbells, Nest Thermostats, Arlo Security Cameras, smart locks, smart lighting, and many other devices.

Can you help fix WiFi dead zones?

Absolutely. We analyze your home's layout and recommend solutions like mesh networks, WiFi extenders, or optimal router placement.

Do you offer same-day service?

We often have same-day or next-day availability. Contact us to check our current schedule.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, Venmo, Zelle, and other common payment methods.
